No boards to groom. No fields to fill. You keep taking notes the way you always have; Nimbus does the filing, the sequencing, and the cutting.

What it does

01 — 03
01

Capture without filing

Dump everything into one inbox — pasted meeting notes, whiteboard photos, half-thoughts at 1 a.m. Nimbus threads related fragments together on its own. You never touch a folder or a tag.

02

Plans that draft themselves

Every morning Nimbus proposes the shortest path from what you wrote to what ships: milestones, dependencies, rough sizes — and a cut list of what to drop. Approve it or drag it around; it re-sorts as reality changes.

03

Ship-state, not status meetings

One live page shows what moved, what's blocked, and what's next — shareable by link, current without anyone updating it. Cancel the Monday sync.

How it works

Three moves
STEP 1

Dump

Forward notes, paste docs, or connect your editor. Raw is fine — raw is the point.

STEP 2

Review the draft

Each morning: one proposed plan, one cut list. Accept, edit, or reject in under a minute.

STEP 3

Ship

Work the first task. Done items retire themselves; the plan stays honest.
